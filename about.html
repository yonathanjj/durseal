Our Story

Duraseal was founded to meet the rising demand for high-quality construction chemical suppliers in Rwanda, particularly in waterproofing and adhesives. As infrastructure rapidly develops across Kigali and beyond, we partnered with Pidilite Industries to bring their renowned Dr. Fixit waterproofing systems and Fevicol bonding solutions to the local market. From the start, we’ve been committed not just to supplying top-tier products but also to ensuring they are applied correctly—for results that truly last.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be Rwanda’s leading provider of construction waterproofing and adhesive solutions by combining premium-quality products with professional application services. We aim to improve construction standards across the country by making world-class waterproofing systems and adhesives accessible, affordable, and correctly applied—one project at a time.

What We Do

Duraseal Limited specializes in the distribution and application of Dr. Fixit waterproofing products and Fevicol adhesives in Rwanda. Our range of construction chemicals protects structures from water leakage, seepage, and structural wear—covering roofs, basements, water tanks, bathrooms, and more. We also supply Fevicol bonding solutions for woodworking, furniture installation, and interior finishing, trusted by professionals across Kigali and the region.

As authorized applicators, we go beyond product supply by offering on-site application services, technical support, and training for construction professionals. Whether you're a contractor, project manager, or homeowner, we ensure you receive the right product for the job—installed the right way. When you need reliable waterproofing solutions in Rwanda or Fevicol adhesives, think Duraseal: your trusted partner in building protection and performance.
